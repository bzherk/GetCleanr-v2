About Us

A family-run cleaning business built on experience, consistency, and doing the job properly.

Our story

Built on real experience

Getcleanr is a family-run cleaning business built on real experience, consistency, and doing the job properly. We’re a mother-son team serving homes, rentals, and businesses across Calgary.

My mother brings over 10 years of hands-on experience in the cleaning industry, having worked independently across everything from high-end residential homes to fast-paced rental turnovers. Over time, she’s developed a strong eye for detail and a standard that doesn’t get rushed.

What sets her apart isn’t just the work itself. It’s what she notices. The small things most cleaners miss are the things we focus on, and that level of care is the standard we bring to every job.

Photo: Getcleanr mother-son team
Why we’re here

Why we started Getcleanr

We built Getcleanr because we saw a gap in how cleaning services were being delivered.

Too many companies prioritize speed and volume over quality, rushing through jobs and missing the details that actually matter. We wanted to do things differently, keeping it simple, consistent, and reliable.

When you book with us, you’re dealing directly with the people doing the work. We don’t rotate crews or treat your space like just another stop on a checklist. We stay until the job is done to our standards.
