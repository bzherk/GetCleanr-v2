How much do Calgary cleaning services cost?

Pricing depends on the size of the property, the condition of the space, and whether you need recurring, deep, move-out, or commercial cleaning. We quote based on the real scope of work.

What is included in a cleaning appointment?

Most appointments cover kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, dusting, vacuuming, and floor cleaning. Deep cleans and move-out cleans can include more detailed work depending on the property.

Do you clean both homes and businesses?

Yes. Getcleanr provides Calgary cleaning services for homes, condos, offices, and other business spaces.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Calgary, Alberta including downtown, NW, SW, SE, and surrounding areas for residential and commercial cleaning.
